Healthy Recipes

1. Protein Smoothie

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 scoop protein powder (whey, plant-based, or your preference)
    • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
    • 1/2 banana
    • 1 tablespoon almond butter
    • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Instructions:
    1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
    2. Adjust almond milk quantity for desired consistency.
  • Calories: Approximately 300 calories
  • Macros: Protein: 25g, Carbohydrates: 20g, Fat: 12g

2. Chicken and Couscous with Vegetables

  • Ingredients:
    • 6 oz boneless, skinless chicken breast
    • 1/2 cup dry couscous
    • Mixed vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes)
    • Olive oil, garlic, herbs for seasoning
  • Instructions:
    1. Grill or pan-cook chicken with desired seasonings.
    2. Prepare couscous according to package instructions.
    3. Sauté mixed vegetables in olive oil and garlic.
    4. Combine chicken, couscous, and vegetables.
  • Calories: Approximately 500 calories
  • Macros: Protein: 45g, Carbohydrates: 45g, Fat: 15g

3. Chia Pudding

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
    • 1 cup almond milk
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Berries or sliced fruits for topping
  • Instructions:
    1. Mix chia seeds, almond milk, and vanilla extract in a jar.
    2. Stir well and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
    3. Top with berries or sliced fruits before serving.
  • Calories: Approximately 200 calories
  • Macros: Protein: 6g, Carbohydrates: 20g, Fat: 12g

Adjust portions based on your dietary needs and consult a nutritionist for personalized advice.
